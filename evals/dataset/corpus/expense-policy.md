# Travel and Expense Policy

## Expense Reports

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date through
the finance portal. Receipts are mandatory for any expense above 50 dollars.
Reports submitted after 60 days are rejected automatically.

## Travel Booking

All business travel must be booked through the corporate travel tool.
Economy class is the default for flights under six hours; business class is
allowed for longer flights with director approval.

## Per Diem

The domestic per diem rate is 65 dollars per day and covers meals and
incidentals. International per diem rates follow the government schedule of
the destination country. Per diem does not require receipts.

## Corporate Cards

Corporate credit cards are issued to employees who travel more than four
times per year. Personal use of corporate cards is prohibited and leads to
card revocation on the second violation.
