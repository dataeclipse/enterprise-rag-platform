# Employee Handbook

## Vacation Policy

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Unused leave can be carried over into the next year, capped at 5 days.
Carried-over days expire on March 31 of the following year.
Absences longer than two consecutive weeks require written approval from a
direct manager at least one month in advance.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week after completing their
probation period. Fully remote arrangements require VP approval and are
reviewed every six months. Remote employees must be reachable during core
hours from 10:00 to 16:00 in the company's home time zone.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is unlimited but requires a medical certificate after the third
consecutive day of absence. Sick days are tracked separately from vacation
and do not reduce the annual leave balance.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary
caregivers receive 4 weeks. Leave can be split into at most two blocks within
the first year after birth or adoption.
